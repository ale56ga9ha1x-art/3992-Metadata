Flicked Tales is a cozy space for readers who love tracking stories, sharing impressions, and discovering their next favorite book.

Add the books you’re reading or planning to read, rate them your own way, and capture how they made you feel. Whether it’s a quick star rating, a short note, or a detailed review, Flicked Tales helps you remember why a story mattered to you. Over time, your library becomes a personal reading journey you can always come back to.

Exploring new books is just as enjoyable. Browse titles, discover recommendations, and build your own bookshelf that grows with your tastes. Finished a book? Mark it as read and watch your progress add up toward simple, motivating goals that make reading feel even more rewarding.

With a clean design and a relaxed pace, Flicked Tales is made for readers who want less clutter and more connection with the stories they love. Read, reflect, and keep your reading life beautifully organized.
